What is Choreland?

Dive into "Choreland" where chores become epic quests. With augmented reality, tidying up is no longer a task, but a thrilling adventure. Clean, play, and conquer with Choreland!

Read More

Features

AR Cleaning Adventure

Choreland employs augmented reality to make cleaning a room an engaging game. Players progress in a virtual world by completing real cleaning tasks, with the app guiding them by turning chores into interactive challenges, such as treating toy pick-up as treasure collection.

Reward and Progress Tracking System

Choreland features a progress-tracking system that rewards completed tasks. Kids enjoy milestone achievements like unlocking levels and gaining items, making chores fun. Parents benefit by monitoring progress, reinforcing responsibility, and offering real-world rewards, seamlessly connecting virtual achievements with reality.

Enhancing User Engagement

To keep the engagement high, the app can offer various themed adventures. One day, kids might be hunting for lost treasure in a pirate world, and the next, they could be in a futuristic city collecting energy sources. This variety ensures that the cleaning experience remains fresh and exciting.
